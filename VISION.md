# Driver Hub — Vision

## The Goal

Become the definitive app for car enthusiasts worldwide. Not a niche tool, not a side project — the platform that every petrolhead, gearhead, and driving addict opens daily. The car culture equivalent of what Strava is to runners, what Discord is to gamers.

**Target: 10 million active users within 3 years of launch.**

---

## Core Pillars

### 1. Your Garage, Your Identity

Your garage is your profile. Every car you own, have owned, or are building tells your story.

- **Full vehicle profiles** — make, model, year, specs, photos, build history
- **Mod tracker** — log every part, every install, with before/after photos and cost tracking
- **Spec auto-fill** — enter a car, get real-world specs instantly from a comprehensive database
- **Build timeline** — visual history of how your car evolved from stock to current state
- **Valuation tracking** — estimated value based on mods, mileage, and market data
- **Multiple garages** — daily driver, weekend car, project car, dream car wishlist
- **3D garage view** — showcase your cars in a virtual showroom others can walk through

### 2. Live Map & Drive Tracking

The killer feature. Know where your crew is, right now.

- **Real-time friend positions** — see who's online, who's driving, who's parked up
- **Live drives** — start a drive, friends see your route in real time, can join mid-drive
- **Drive recording** — GPS route, speed data, elevation, duration, distance — automatic
- **Drive replays** — watch back any drive on the map with telemetry overlay
- **Heat maps** — see the most popular roads in your area, discover new routes
- **Road ratings** — community-rated roads by scenery, surface quality, twistiness, traffic
- **Scenic route discovery** — AI-recommended drives based on your preferences, time, and location
- **Hazard alerts** — speed cameras, police, road closures, shared by the community in real time
- **Convoy mode** — group drive with voice chat, shared music, leader-follow navigation
- **Mount mode** — landscape heads-up display optimised for phone mount while driving
- **Drive challenges** — weekly featured routes, seasonal road trips, community-created routes

### 3. Social & Community

Car culture is tribal. The app should feel like your crew, not a corporate platform.

- **Feed** — posts, photos, videos, garage shares, drive completions, build updates
- **Stories** — 24-hour ephemeral content from meets, drives, and garage sessions
- **Crews** — create or join groups (JDM crew, M Power gang, local meets, track day regulars)
- **Events** — create, discover, RSVP to local meets, car shows, track days, road trips
- **Forums** — deep-dive discussions: tech help, modifications, photo spots, buy & sell
- **Direct messaging** — 1:1 and group chats with media sharing
- **Voice channels** — always-on voice rooms for crews (like Discord)
- **Car spotting** — snap a rare car in the wild, tag location, community identifies it
- **Marketplace integration** — buy/sell parts, accessories, and cars within your network

### 4. Progression & Gamification

The reason people come back every day.

#### Driver Level System
- **XP earned from:** completing drives, posting content, helping in forums, attending events, logging mods, receiving likes, daily login streaks
- **Driver ranks:** from "Learner" to "Road Legend" — visible on your profile and in the map
- **Seasonal rankings** — monthly and quarterly leaderboards reset, keeping competition fresh

#### Achievements & Badges
- **Drive badges:** "Canyon Carver" (50 mountain drives), "Night Owl" (100 drives after dark), "Cross-Country" (drive 500+ miles in one trip)
- **Social badges:** "Crew Chief" (lead 10 convoys), "Mentor" (50 solved forum posts), "Event Host" (organise 5 meets)
- **Garage badges:** "Collector" (5+ cars), "Builder" (20+ mods logged), "Purist" (stock car, 10k miles driven)
- **Rare badges:** "First Drive" (drove a new road before anyone else rated it), "Sunrise Run" (start a drive before 5am), "Full Send" (complete a 12-hour drive)
- **Car-specific badges:** "Rotary Faithful", "V8 Thunder", "EV Pioneer", "JDM Legend", "Euro Elite"

#### Streaks & Challenges
- **Daily streaks** — consecutive days with activity (drive, post, or forum interaction)
- **Weekly challenges** — "Drive 100 miles this week", "Post 3 photos", "Rate 5 roads"
- **Monthly events** — themed challenges (January: "New Year, New Roads", Summer: "Road Trip Season")
- **Friend challenges** — challenge a friend to a specific route, compare times and telemetry

#### Reputation System
- **Rep score** — earned from community contributions, quality content, and helpfulness
- **Rep tiers** unlock perks: custom profile frames, exclusive themes, early access to features
- **Forum authority** — high-rep users can mark answers as solved, moderate threads
- **Verified builders** — users with documented build quality get a verified wrench badge

### 5. Themes & Personalisation

The app should feel like YOUR app, not everyone else's.

- **Hand-crafted themes** — each with unique typography, colours, gradients, animations, and sound design
- **Starter themes:**
  - Most Wanted — street racing legacy, black and amber, hazard stripes
  - Horizon — festival vibes, deep blue, cyan glow
  - Midnight — clean minimal, dark purple, understated
  - M Power — BMW Motorsport, blue/purple/red tri-stripe
- **Unlockable themes** — earned through progression (JDM Neon, GT Racing, Rally Dirt, Luxury, Retro 80s)
- **Car brand themes** — official partnerships (Porsche, Ferrari, Mercedes-AMG, Nissan NISMO)
- **Custom themes** — power users can create and share their own colour schemes
- **Seasonal themes** — limited-time themes for events (Christmas, Halloween, Le Mans week)
- **Sound packs** — notification sounds themed to engine types (V8 rumble, turbo flutter, EV whine)
- **App icon selection** — choose from themed app icons to match your style

## Platform Features

### Analytics Dashboard
- **Drive stats** — total miles, drives, average speed, favourite roads, time spent driving
- **Monthly/yearly reports** — "Your Year in Driving" wrapped-style summary
- **Garage analytics** — money spent on mods, value added, build completion percentage
- **Social stats** — engagement, follower growth, most popular posts

### Smart Notifications
- **Friend started a drive nearby** — tap to join
- **New event near you** — meets, shows, track days
- **Someone replied to your forum post**
- **Your car's service reminder** — based on mileage tracking
- **Road condition alerts** — for your saved favourite routes
- **Streak at risk** — gentle nudge to keep your streak alive

### Safety & Privacy
- **Ghost mode** — hide your location from the map temporarily
- **Drive-only mode** — minimal UI, no notifications, just the map and speed
- **Passenger mode** — full app access when you're not the driver
- **Automatic drive detection** — app knows when you're driving and switches to safe mode
- **Location sharing controls** — per-friend visibility settings
- **Data export** — download all your data anytime

### Integrations
- **OBD2 / car telemetry** — connect to your car for real-time data (RPM, boost, temps)
- **Apple CarPlay / Android Auto** — map and drive tracking on your car's screen
- **Spotify / Apple Music** — share what you're listening to on drives
- **Insurance partnerships** — good driving data could lower premiums
- **Workshop finder** — locate trusted mechanics rated by the community

## Monetisation (Future)

- **Free tier** — full social features, garage, basic map, forums
- **Pro tier** — advanced telemetry, unlimited drive history, detailed analytics, exclusive themes, priority event RSVPs
- **Theme store** — premium and brand-partnership themes
- **Event promotion** — paid visibility for commercial events and shows
- **No ads in feed. Ever.** The community is the product, not the user's attention.

---

## Technical Vision

- **React Native + Expo** — single codebase for Android and iOS
- **Real-time backend** — WebSockets for live map, presence, and messaging
- **Edge-first architecture** — fast globally, not just locally
- **Offline support** — drive tracking works without signal, syncs when back online
- **Push notifications** — real-time alerts for social and drive events
- **Background location** — accurate drive tracking even when app is backgrounded
- **Scalable to millions** — designed from day one to handle global load
