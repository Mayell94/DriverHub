# Driver Hub

A social platform built for driving enthusiasts. Track your garage, connect with friends on the road, and share the culture.

## Vision

Car culture lives on back roads, in group chats, and at midnight meets — not on generic social media. Driver Hub is built specifically for people who care about cars. It's the app you open before a drive, during a meet, and after a build.

**What Driver Hub is:**
- A virtual garage where you catalogue your cars, track mods, and show them off
- A live map that shows where your friends are driving right now
- A community feed built around cars, not algorithms
- Forums for real help — tech questions, mod advice, route recommendations
- Messaging that feels like a crew group chat, not a corporate inbox

**What Driver Hub is not:**
- A marketplace (though parts get traded in the forums)
- A racing game
- Another generic social network with a car skin on top

## Features

### Virtual Garage
Add your cars with real-world specs auto-filled from a built-in database. Track every modification, build a photo gallery, and share your garage to the feed.

### Live Map
See where your crew is in real time. Spot who's driving, who's online, and join live drives. Landscape mode turns your phone into a mount-friendly heads-up display.

### Community Feed
Post updates, share garage galleries, like and comment. No algorithmic timeline — just your people, in order.

### Forums
Categories for tech help, modifications, photo spots, meets & events, and buy & sell. Threads are marked hot or solved so you find answers fast.

### Friends & Messaging
See who's online, who's out driving, and message them directly. Invite friends to drives straight from the map.

### Themes
Four hand-crafted visual themes inspired by car culture:

| Theme | Vibe |
|-------|------|
| **Most Wanted** | Street racing legacy — black and amber with hazard stripes |
| **Horizon** | Festival vibes — deep blue with cyan accents |
| **Midnight** | Clean and minimal — dark purple, understated |
| **M Power** | BMW Motorsport heritage — blue, purple, red tri-stripe |

Each theme has its own typography, colour palette, and visual details.

## Tech Stack

- **React Native** + **Expo SDK 54** — cross-platform for Android and iOS
- **TypeScript** — strict mode
- **expo-router** — file-based navigation
- **react-native-maps** — live map with friend tracking
- **react-native-reanimated** — smooth animations
- **expo-linear-gradient** — theme gradients

## Getting Started

```bash
# Prerequisites: Node.js 22+
npm install
npx expo start
```

Scan the QR code with Expo Go on your phone, or press `a` for Android emulator.

## Project Status

Early development. Migrating from a working web prototype to native mobile. The original prototype (`driver-hub.jsx`) is included as a reference for the full feature set and design language.
